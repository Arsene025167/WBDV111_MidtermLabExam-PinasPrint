2. Refund Policy

Due to the customized nature of printing services, refunds are subject to the conditions below:

2.1 Non-Refundable Cases

  • Change of mind after production has started
  • Errors approved by the client during proof confirmation
  • Color variations within acceptable printing tolerances

2.2 Refundable / Reprint Cases

  • Defective or damaged items upon delivery
  • Incorrect quantity or specifications caused by PinasPrint
  • Severe printing errors not approved by the client

Claims must be reported within 48 hours of receiving the order, with supporting photos or videos.

💡 Approved refunds may be issued as reprints, store credits, or monetary refunds depending on the situation.

3. Shipping Policy

PinasPrint delivers nationwide within the Philippines through trusted courier partners.

3.1 Processing Time

  • Standard orders: 3–7 business days
  • Bulk or custom projects: subject to quotation and approval

3.2 Delivery Time

  • Metro Manila: 1–3 business days
  • Provincial areas: 3–7 business days

3.3 Shipping Responsibility

Once orders are handed over to the courier, delivery timelines are subject to courier policies and external conditions.
